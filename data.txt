
This file describes steps necessary to load data

============

Data is available from http://mta.info/developers/turnstile.html

============

# 1. Loading Remote-Booth-Station.xls

open in Excel or Numbers and export to csv
use scripts/upload_remote_booth_data.py to
upload into the appengine instance

    # upload locally
    appcfg.py upload_data --config_file=lib/models.py --filename=data/Remote-Booth-Station.csv --kind=Unit --url=http://127.0.0.1:8086/remote_api --application=mta-turnstiles
    # upload remotely
    appcfg.py upload_data --config_file=lib/models.py --filename=data/Remote-Booth-Station.csv --kind=Unit --application=mta-turnstiles .

# 2. Loading daily data

* Download the daily zip file
* extract the zip
* scripts/turnstile_to_csv.py --file=data/turnstile_20100505.txt
* appcfg.py upload_data --config_file=bulkloader.yaml --filename=data/turnstile_20100505.csv --kind=Unit .
